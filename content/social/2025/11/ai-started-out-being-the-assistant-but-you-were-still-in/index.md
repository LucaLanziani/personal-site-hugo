"AI started out being the assistant, but you were still in control ... But now it’s switching around. The AI is doing the work, right? And you’re supervising what it’s doing."

<!--more-->

- Anders Hejlsberg (creator of TypeScript)
In a recent short interview about TypeScript, the role of Open Source, and the perfect language for AI, Anders was asked:
How has AI changed the developer workflow?
His answer points to a fundamental shift, ending with this prediction:
"That’s going to change development tools dramatically."
I'm not sure if this vision will fully come true, but I would be sad if I have to give up my beloved editor.
What do you think? Are our tools ready for this new "supervisor" role?
Do you even want to become a mere "supervisor"?
Full video (where he also discusses TypeScript, Go, and OSS) is in the first comment.
